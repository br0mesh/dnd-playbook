# Mature Rat

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 3
**Wave:** Sewer or cellar
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)
**Stat source:** Homebrew (MM Giant Rat +)

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Hold chokepoints in pairs; flanking rats use Pack Tactics while one draws attention.
**Flee:** When half the group is down, survivors scatter into cracks.

---

### Stat Block

**Type:** Small beast, unaligned
**Armor Class AC:** 13
**Hit Points HP:** 11 (2d8 + 2)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages:** —
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)

### Traits

- **Keen Smell.** Advantage on Perception checks that rely on smell.
- **Pack Tactics.** Advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one ally is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### Actions

| Action | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bite | +4 | 1d6+2 piercing | Melee, 5 ft. |

### DM Notes

- Use 2–3 for a level-1 party of 4; tougher than MM **Giant Rat** (AC 12, 7 HP, 1d4+2). Pair with **03_swarm_of_rats_monster** if the lair feels overcrowded.
- Mature rats are fully grown sewer or granary stock — thicker hide and colony fighting, without regional variants.
- Highlight tight quarters once: half cover from crates, or difficult terrain from filth.
