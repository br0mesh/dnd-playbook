# Ray of Frost

### Header

**Name:** Ray of Frost
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Evocation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

Ranged spell attack, 1d8 cold damage; speed reduced by 10 ft. until start of your next turn on a hit.
