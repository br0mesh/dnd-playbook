# Wand of the War Mage +1

### Header

**Name:** Wand of the War Mage +1
**Rarity:** Uncommon
**Type:** Wand

### Properties

**Attunement:** Required (spellcaster)
**Weight:** 1 lb.

### Description

While holding this wand, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls. The wand has 7 charges for magic missile (1 charge per casting). The wand stores Magic Missile.
