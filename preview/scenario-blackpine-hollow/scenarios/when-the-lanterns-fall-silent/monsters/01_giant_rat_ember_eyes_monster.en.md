# Giant Rat (Ember Eyes)

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 3
**Wave:** Mill Rat Problem: Garrick Moss's Wheel
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/8 (25 XP)
**Stat source:** MM

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Charge from the culvert as a unit toward the wheel shadow; one squeak and they turn together. Keen Smell per MM.
**Flee:** When combined rat HP is at or below half starting total; or at 1 HP each if the fight goes badly (Garrick may throw a rock).

### DM Notes

- Scene: `10_mill_rat_problem` — optional combat tutorial at Garrick Moss's mill.
- **Scaling:** 3 for 4–5 PCs (default); 2 for 3 PCs; 4–5 if the table wants a longer tutorial.
- **Cosmetic only:** faint glowing ember eyes; coordinated swarm movement — no stat changes from MM baseline.
- **Ambient wrongness:** rats touched by regional wrongness — not necromancer minions; no lecture needed.
- Highlight difficult terrain (mud) once during movement.
- **Non-combat escape:** Stealth (DC 12) or Animal Handling (DC 13) to scatter rats — still find tracks with Investigation DC 10.
- **If fight drags:** rats flee when combined HP ≤ half starting total; Garrick helps with one rock attack if party struggles.
