# Herbalist's House: Silas Thornwick's Diary

**Type:** Core clue scene · quiet investigation · **Pacing:** ~20–25 minutes

### Summary

**Purpose:** Deliver the **strongest forest lead** — **Silas Thornwick** is missing, his house undisturbed, his **diary** points to lights **answering each other** along the **old forest trail**. Attach **volunteer stablehand rumor** (Scene 7 layer).

**Ends when:** The party has the **diary excerpt** (and optional herb maps), hears the **stablehand rumor**, and knows the source is **outside the village** — ready to consolidate in `11_mayor_followup`.

**Setting:** Mid-morning. **Silas Thornwick's house** — herb bundles drying, quiet kettle, door unlocked. No combat unless players invent it.

### Read-aloud

**Approach**

> The herbalist's lane smells of mugwort and wet slate. **Silas Thornwick's** door stands unlocked — he trusted neighbors, or left in a hurry without fear of burglars. Inside: tidy chaos of jars, pressed leaves, a cot still made. **No blood.** **No struggle.** Only the sense that someone walked out and the house is **waiting**.

**Stablehand beat (Scene 7 — volunteer trail)**

> A boy mucking the **stable** across the lane wipes his hands. "You're for the mayor? Last week's **volunteers** — three of them — asked which house was Silas's. They went inside at **dusk**, came out with packs tight, headed **toward the forest trail**. Haven't seen **them** since either."

### Checks

**Investigation (DC 12)** — Find diary and notes (one meaningful search)

| Result | Find |
|--------|------|
| **Fail** | Obvious clutter — grant **automatic success** if players search **desk**, **bed**, or **hearth** specifically (meaningful choice). |
| **Success** | **Leather diary** under desk blotter; last entry **three nights ago**. |
| **Success by 5+** | **Forest herb route map** pinned inside cover — old trail marked with three X's "where lights gathered." |

**Nature or Survival (DC 11)** — Herb routes on map

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Success** | X marks match **game trails**, not village paths — same direction as Coyle tracks in `07`. |
| **Fail** | Map still shows trail mouth — enough to commit. |

**Arcana (DC 12)** — Diary language ("answering each other")

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Success** | Reads like **broadcast behaviour** — lights **signal**, not wander. Matches Lilith's carrier-wave sketch if shared. |
| **Success by 5+** | Faint **necromancy undertone** in word choice only on DC 14 — DM may hint "wrong kind of answering" without naming souls. |

**Diary excerpt (handout — read when found):**

> Third night of the blue lights. They are not fireflies, not will-o'-wisps, and not lanterns carried by human hands. They move as if answering each other.
>
> The village lanterns flickered again tonight, always when the forest lights gathered near the old trail.
>
> I will follow at dawn. If I am right, the source is not in the village.

**Perception (DC 13)** — While reading

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Success** | Through window: **single blue flicker** in treeline — **one beat**, then gone. No chase required. |
| **Fail** | Quiet house — sparrow on sill. |

#### Branch A — Thorough search

**Option 1 — Pack Silas's field kit**

- **Investigation (DC 10):** Rope, tinder, **partial herb kit** — he planned **one** trip, not exile.
- **Option 2 — Copy map only**
- Faster exit — still satisfies forest lead.

#### Branch B — Quick search (time short)

- One **Investigation** at desk → diary automatic on success or meaningful choice.
- Skip stablehand if already heard volunteer rumor in `06`.

**Clues to carry forward:**

- Forest lights move in **pattern** — village reacts when forest gathers at **old trail**.
- **Silas followed at dawn** — did not return.
- **Volunteers** came here last — same trail.
- Source is **outside Briarford** — mandatory for `11` commit.

**Convergence hooks:**

- **Elias "dead light" journal** ↔ Silas "not fireflies, not will-o'-wisps."
- **Lilith pulse / breath-rhythm** ↔ "when forest lights gathered."
- **Mordain "Dead light sees the road"** ↔ Fenwick's verse + Silas's trail X's.
- **Volunteer initials** (`07`) ↔ stablehand saw them leave after this house.

### Contingencies

- **If party skipped stablehand:** Fenwick or Alden confirms in `11` — "They asked about Silas before they left."
- **If Investigation fails repeatedly:** Diary **open on desk** — Silas left in haste; do not block session.
- **If they steal all herbs:** Fenwick scolds at `11` — mechanical penalty optional; diary matters more.
- **If they camp in house:** Rest allowed — **`11`** still needs explicit forest decision.

### DM Notes

- **Pacing:** 20–25 minutes — quiet, unsettling, **no** combat default.
- **Mandatory:** **Yes** for forest commit — if table is out of time, run this **instead of** mill or fakes.
- **Silas Thornwick:** Soul-lantern candidate later — do **not** foreshadow beyond diary.
- **Truth to hide:** Lights are **bound souls** — diary describes behaviour only.
- **Tone:** Respectful silence — players should **want** to find him.
- **Next scene:** `10_mill_rat_problem` if chosen and time; else `11_mayor_followup`.
