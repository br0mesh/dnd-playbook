# Ginger Tom — Ruta's Cat

---

### Scene Reference

**Default quantity:** 1
**Appears in:** 05_intro_ruta, 06_mayors_hall_convergence, 10_mill_rat_problem
**Challenge Rating CR:** 0 (10 XP; ally — MM Cat)
**Stat source:** MM Cat

### Quick Roleplay

**Voice:** Silent except yowls, chirps, and offended silence; communicates by bonking wrists and stealing food
**Goal:** Keep Ruta fed (by stealing her dinner); flee anything that smells like wrong blue light — except rats, which are personal enemies
**Behavior:** Reacts to blue pulses one beat before human senses; optional companion if Ruta succeeds **Animal Handling** in `05`; not magical

### Sample Lines

| Line | When | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *(Bonks Ruta's wrist once, contractually, then eats her fish.)* | `05` friend ending | Sets tone — ally by choice, not command |
| *(Yowls at empty air; fur rises; stares at culvert before PCs roll Perception.)* | Blue pulse or rat fight | Animals nervous first — no speech |
| *(Sits on hall steps washing a paw as if he owns the appointment.)* | `06` if befriended | Flavour beat only |

### DM Notes

- **Ally rules:** If befriended in `05`, the tom fights at **`10_mill_rat_problem`** only when Ruta is present and the player wants him in — roll **his own initiative** or share Ruta's turn (table choice; shared turn is easier for a tutorial). **Stat block:** monster sheet **`04_ginger_tom_cat_monster`**.
- **Combat role:** **Help** on one ally's attack against a rat per round (advantage); **Claws** only if a rat is in adjacent square and Ruta's player asks for it. He is not a damage dealer.
- **Flee:** At **1 HP**, or if **three or more** hostile rats can reach him on their next turn — dashes for a wall gap; Ruta can **Animal Handling (DC 10)** on her turn to call him back next round.
- **Sense beat:** Before rat combat at the mill, he **yowls** at the culvert — grants Ruta (or one PC she designates) **advantage on the first Perception check** to spot ember-eyed rats charging.
- **Not for:** Boss fights, necromancer scenes, or PvP — comedy and tutorial only.
- **If Ruta failed Animal Handling in `05`:** He may still appear on the **inn porch** overnight; he does **not** join mill combat unless befriended later (**Animal Handling DC 13** at the mill door).
- Do **not** grant spell-like abilities or blue-light immunity — he **reacts** early, nothing more.
