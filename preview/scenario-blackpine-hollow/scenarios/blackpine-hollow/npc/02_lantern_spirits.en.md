# Lantern Spirits

---

### Scene Reference

**Default quantity:** 5
**Appears in:** Scene 1 hook through epilogue
**Challenge Rating CR:** 0 (non-combat)
**Stat source:** Homebrew

### Quick Roleplay

**Voice:** None — communicate by light and motion only
**Goal:** Lead party to shrine; witness Mosswick's fate
**Behavior:** Calm pulse when party cooperates; blue flicker when danger or sadness appears

### Sample Lines

| Line | When | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Slow pulse toward forest trail | Party hesitates at camp | Invitation to follow |
| Quick flicker, drift back | Player insults the forest | Warning |
| Five lights in a line | Blue campfire hook | Scene 1 lead into woods |
| Circle shrine silently | Mosswick confrontation | Witness, no speech |
| One light dips over fire | Epilogue | Calm return to camp |

---

### Stat Block

**Type:** Tiny fey spirit, neutral good
**Speed:** fly 30 ft.
**Languages:** —

### DM Notes

- **Appearance:** Small golden lights, size of campfire sparks; drift in lines and circles and never speak clearly.
- **Slow pulsing:** Calm, safe — the party is on the right path.
- **Quick flickering:** Warning — danger, a bad choice, or Mosswick's influence nearby.
- **Floating closer:** Invitation to follow toward the next scene.
- **Turning blue:** Sadness or danger — shrine grief, trapped fey, or party cruelty.
- **Shrine witness:** Circle silently during the Mosswick confrontation; brighten when players share food or speak kindly at camp.
- **Epilogue:** One lantern dips over the fire, then rises toward the stars.
