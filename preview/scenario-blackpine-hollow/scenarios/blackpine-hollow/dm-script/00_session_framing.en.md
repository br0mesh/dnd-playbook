# Session Framing

### Summary

Use this scene to set expectations before the campfire opening. Blackpine Hollow is a beginner-friendly level 3 one-shot with a cozy, spooky, funny, mysterious tone. The main goal is simple: follow the lantern spirits to the ruined shrine and decide what to do with Mosswick.

Key NPC reminders:
- **Mosswick** is unsettling, lonely, playful, and not purely evil.
- **Lantern spirits** do not speak clearly, but they communicate emotion through movement and light.

### Read-aloud

> This is a beginner-friendly one-shot. You can try creative things, and I will tell you what to roll if a roll is needed. If I do not know a rule, I will make a quick fair ruling and we will keep playing. The goal is to have fun and tell a spooky campfire story together.
>
> Before we begin, answer three questions:
> 1. What is your character doing around the campfire?
> 2. What food, song, or story did your character bring?
> 3. Which other character do you already trust?

### Checks

No rolls are needed in this scene.

If players ask for guidance, keep the answers simple:
- **What level are we?** Level 3.
- **Do we know each other already?** Your choice.
- **What is tonight about?** A strange campfire mystery that leads into the forest.

### Contingencies

- **If a player is shy:** Offer an easier version of the first question: are you cooking, resting, telling a story, or keeping watch?
- **If the table stalls:** Move straight into Scene 1 once everyone has said one thing.
- **If rules questions pile up:** Give a short answer now and teach the details only when they matter in play.

### DM Notes

- Keep this opening light, welcoming, and fast.
- The core pitch for the whole session is: lantern spirits, ruined shrine, Mosswick's fate.
- Use the lantern spirits to keep pacing moving whenever the table gets stuck.
- After everyone speaks once, begin `01_camping_clearing`.
