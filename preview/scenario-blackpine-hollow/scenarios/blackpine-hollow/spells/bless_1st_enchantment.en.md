# Bless

### Header

**Name:** Bless
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Enchantment

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Components:** V, S, M (a sprinkling of holy water)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

### Description

Bless up to three creatures of your choice within range. Whenever a target makes an attack roll or saving throw before the spell ends, it can add 1d4 to the roll.

### At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
