# Eldritch Blast

### Header

**Name:** Eldritch Blast
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Evocation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 120 ft.
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

A beam of crackling energy streaks toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 force damage.

### At Higher Levels

The spell creates more than one beam at higher character levels: two at 5th level, three at 11th, and four at 17th. You can direct the beams at the same target or different ones, making a separate attack roll for each beam.
