# Goodberry

### Header

**Name:** Goodberry
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Transmutation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a sprig of mistletoe)
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

Up to ten berries appear in your hand and are infused with magic for 24 hours. A creature can use an action to eat one berry, regaining 1 hit point. A berry also provides enough nourishment to sustain a creature for one day.
