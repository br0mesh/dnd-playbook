# Spiritual Weapon

### Header

**Name:** Spiritual Weapon
**Level:** 2nd
**School:** Evocation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 bonus action
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 minute

### Description

Create a floating spectral weapon within range. When you cast the spell, make a melee spell attack against a creature within 5 feet of the weapon. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier force damage. On later turns, you can use a bonus action to move the weapon up to 20 feet and repeat the attack.

### At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for every two slot levels above 2nd.
