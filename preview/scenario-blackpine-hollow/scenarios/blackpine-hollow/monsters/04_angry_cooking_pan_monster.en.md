# Angry Cooking Pan

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 1-2
**Wave:** Optional camp prank / Mosswick chaos
**Challenge Rating CR:** —
**Stat source:** Homebrew

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Targets whoever insulted dinner; bonks and flies behind cover.
**Counterplay:** Dexterity or Strength DC 13 to grab it.
