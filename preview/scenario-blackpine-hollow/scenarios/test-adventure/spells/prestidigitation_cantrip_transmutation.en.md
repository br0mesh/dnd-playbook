# Prestidigitation

### Header

**Name:** Prestidigitation
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Transmutation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 10 ft
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 hour

### Description

Minor magical trick: harmless sensory effect, light/snuff candle/torch, clean/soil 1 cu ft object, chill/warm/wet 1 lb material, mark/symbol, or create small trinket.
