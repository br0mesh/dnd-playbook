# Rod of Lordly Might

### Header

**Name:** Rod of Lordly Might
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Type:** Rod

### Properties

**Attunement:** Required
**Weight:** 2 lb.

### Description

This rod can be a +3 mace, +3 sword, or +3 battleaxe. Press buttons to extend a climbing pole (20 ft), open doors, or paralyze a creature (DC 17 STR save) for 1 minute (1/day).
