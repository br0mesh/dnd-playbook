# Staff of the Adder

### Header

**Name:** Staff of the Adder
**Rarity:** Uncommon
**Type:** Staff

### Properties

**Attunement:** Required (druid, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard)
**Weight:** 4 lb.

### Description

Use an action to make a melee spell attack with the staff (+5 to hit). On a hit, the target takes 1d6+2 piercing damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or take 3d6 poison damage.
