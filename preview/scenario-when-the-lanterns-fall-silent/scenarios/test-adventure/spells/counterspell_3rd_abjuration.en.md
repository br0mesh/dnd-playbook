# Counterspell

### Header

**Name:** Counterspell
**Level:** 3rd
**School:** Abjuration

### Casting

**Time:** 1 reaction
**Range:** 60 ft
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

Reaction when you see a creature casting a spell within range. If spell level ≤ 3, it fails. Higher level: ability check (DC 10 + spell level) or the spell fails.
