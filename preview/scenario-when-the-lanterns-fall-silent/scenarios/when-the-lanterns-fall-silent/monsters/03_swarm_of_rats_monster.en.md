# Swarm of Rats

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 1
**Wave:** Collapsed culvert or forest-edge burrow
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)
**Stat source:** MM

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Surge from the culvert as one mass; occupy a PC's space and focus the nearest target.
**Flee:** At half HP, the swarm breaks apart and vanishes into mud cracks or the wheel shadow.

---

### Stat Block

**Type:** Medium swarm of Tiny beasts, unaligned
**Armor Class AC:** 10
**Hit Points HP:** 24 (7d8 − 7)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Senses:** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages:** —
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)
**Damage Resistances:** bludgeoning, piercing, slashing
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, prone, restrained, stunned

### Traits

- **Swarm.** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny rat. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

### Actions

| Action | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|

### DM Notes

- Scene: `10_mill_rat_problem` — **harder tutorial** option: 1 swarm instead of 3 ember-eyed rats; or swarm + 1 **`01_giant_rat_ember_eyes_monster`** if the table wants a spike without a long fight.
- **Ambient wrongness:** optional faint ember glints in the mass — cosmetic only; no stat change.
- Tracks after the fight still lead toward **forest edge** (Mara Kell) per `10` read-aloud.
- Area damage and shove-prone tactics teach more than single-target basics — good if the party already knows attack rolls.
- **If fight drags:** at half swarm HP, describe rats peeling away into the culvert; Garrick may help with one rock attack.
