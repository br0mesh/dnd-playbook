# Mature Rat

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 3
**Wave:** Mill culvert, granary, or Briarford cellars
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)
**Stat source:** Homebrew (MM Giant Rat +)

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Hold the culvert mouth in pairs; flanking rats use Pack Tactics while one draws attention.
**Flee:** When half the group is down, survivors bolt into wall gaps or the culvert.

---

- **Pack Tactics.** Advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one ally is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### DM Notes

- Tougher than **`01_giant_rat_ember_eyes_monster`** (CR 1/8) — fat granary stock, thicker hide, heavier bite. **No ember eyes**, no coordinated swarm turn.
- Scene: `10_mill_rat_problem` — use **2 mature + 1 ember eyes** for a mixed tutorial, or **2–3 mature** alone if you want a harder fight without the wrongness beat.
- **Scaling:** 3 for 4–5 PCs at level 1; 2 for 3 PCs; add a 4th mature rat only if the table wants pressure.
- Highlight difficult terrain (mud) once during movement.
- **Non-combat escape:** Stealth (DC 13) or Animal Handling (DC 14) to scatter — slightly harder than ember-eyed rats in `10`.
