# Sleep

### Header

**Name:** Sleep
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Enchantment

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 90 ft.
**Components:** V, S, M
**Duration:** 1 minute

### Description

Roll 5d8; creatures in a 20-ft. radius with lowest current HP fall asleep until damage or someone uses an action to wake them.
