# Forest Edge Closing: Blue Light Answers

**Type:** Session end · cliffhanger only · **Pacing:** ~5 minutes

### Summary

**Purpose:** End session 1 with **momentum into session 2** — party commits at the **old forest trail**, village lanterns **dim behind them**, **blue light answers** ahead. **No** new investigation loop.

**Ends when:** You read the closing image and stop — session 2 picks up **on the trail** or **first steps into the trees**.

**Prerequisite:** `11_mayor_followup` forest commit — party rested, map in hand, at least **two** forest clues locked.

### Read-aloud

**Trail mouth**

> Predawn grey. **Fenwick's sketch** crinkles in someone's pack. Behind you, Briarford's chimneys show first smoke. Ahead, the **old forest trail** narrows under arching briars — the same mouth **Silas** marked, **Mara** walked, **Widow Coyle** followed when her porch light lied.
>
> You are not heroes yet. You are **hirelings** who ran out of village answers.

**Closing image (verbatim stop point)**

> As the party reaches the edge of the trees, the village lanterns behind them **dim one by one**. In the forest ahead, a **blue light answers**.

**Hold there.** Do not roll initiative. Do not describe the light's source.

### Checks

No required checks. Optional color only:

| Optional roll | If used |
|---------------|---------|
| **Perception (DC 12)** | Count **three** village porch lights dimming — not all — pattern not random. |
| **Religion or Arcana (DC 13)** | The answering blue **pulses once** like **breath** (**Elias / Lilith** callback) — then scene ends. |
| **Wisdom save (DC 10)** | Unease only — no damage; fail = shiver, not fear effect. |

### Contingencies

- **If party hesitates:** Remind — volunteers, Silas, Coyle; village behind; job unpaid.
- **If they turn back:** Briarford lanterns **hold yellow** — push is narrative; allow return to inn if table not ready (extend `11`, do not punish).
- **If they charge the light:** **Stop session** mid-step — "We'll find out next time."
- **If session ran long:** Skip optional rolls — read closing paragraph only.

### DM Notes

- **Pacing:** **5 minutes max** — one paragraph plus optional single roll.
- **Mandatory:** Yes as session 1 end — **only** after forest commit.
- **Truth to hide:** Answering light is **soul-lantern network** — describe **colour and pulse** only.
- **Intro echo:** Name-whisper, divine pull, cat's hiss, diary words — **paid off emotionally**, not mechanically here.
- **Session 2 entry:** Trail travel, hunter notes, coal mine — do not preview in detail.
- **Mine lantern:** If awarded in `10`, it **does not** outshine the forest blue — normal `light_cantrip_evocation` is warm by contrast.
