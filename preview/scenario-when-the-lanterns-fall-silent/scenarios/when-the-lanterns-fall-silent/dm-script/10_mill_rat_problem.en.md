# Mill Rat Problem: Garrick Moss's Wheel *(Optional)*

**Type:** Optional combat tutorial · **Location:** Garrick Moss's mill · **Pacing:** ~15–20 minutes

### Summary

**Purpose:** Teach **initiative, movement, and attacks** with a **short, beginner-friendly** fight — without replacing investigation. **Mara Kell** (missing hunter) left **strange tracks** behind the mill toward the forest edge. Optional reward: **old mine lantern** (rechargeable with `light_cantrip_evocation`).

**Ends when:** Rats are driven off or killed, tracks noted, party has miller's thanks and optional lantern — then returns to `11_mayor_followup`.

**Skip if:** Table is low on time or wants investigation-only session 1 — say so in Summary at table.

### Read-aloud

**Mill yard**

> **Garrick Moss's mill** groans — wheel turning slow, flour dust on everything. Garrick himself — flour in his beard, hands shaking — meets you at the door.
>
> "You're not **Mara**. She went to the **herbalist** first, then said she'd check the **back culvert**. That was **four days ago**. Now I've got **rats** — big ones — eyes like embers, gnawing in a **spiral** on the post. I can't go back there alone."

**Behind the mill (before or after fight)**

> Mud and tail-drag marks. **Boot prints** — Mara Kell's sized — overlap rat trails heading **toward the forest edge**, not the village square.

### Checks

**Survival or Investigation (DC 11)** — Tracks behind mill

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Fail** | Rats everywhere — muddy, unclear. |
| **Success** | **Spiral gnaw marks** on post — not normal rodent hunger; tracks lead **past** the culvert toward **trees**. |
| **Success by 5+** | Mara's **chalk tick** on a stone — arrow toward **old forest trail** (matches `09` map). |

**Combat — rat encounter**

Use monster sheet **`01_giant_rat_ember_eyes_monster`** — default **3** for 4–5 PCs; **2** for 3 PCs; **4–5** if table wants longer tutorial. Cosmetic: **faint glowing eyes**, coordinated **swarm** movement toward the wheel shadow.

| Teaching beat | DM tip |
|---------------|--------|
| **Initiative** | "Roll initiative — who acts first?" |
| **Movement** | Highlight difficult terrain (mud) once |
| **Attack roll** | Walk through one hit and one miss |
| **Damage** | Rats flee at **half party HP** total among rats if fight drags |

**Read-aloud when rats charge:**

> Three shapes bolt from the culvert — rats big as cats, eyes catching light like **bad embers**. They move together, not scattered — one squeak and they **turn** as a unit.

**After combat**

> Garrick crosses himself. "Mara said the **forest side** was wrong. Take this — mine lantern from my granddad. I don't go underground anymore."

**Old mine lantern (reward — prose item):**

- Brass **mine lantern** — hooded, dented, **no** magic until charged.
- A caster may recharge it once per day by casting **`light_cantrip_evocation`** into the wick — behaves as normal lantern light for 1 hour (DM adjudiates; not a listed magic item slug).
- **Arcana or Perception (DC 12):** Scratches inside hood — **coal mine** mark; foreshadows session 2.

**Clues to carry forward:**

- **Mara Kell** followed **herbalist / forest** lead past the mill.
- Tracks toward **forest edge** — satisfies `11` minimum with `07` or `09`.
- Mine lantern — optional darkness prep later.

### Contingencies

- **If party skips mill:** Mara tracks available via `09` map + `07` post — forest commit still valid.
- **If fight goes badly:** Garrick helps — one **rock** attack; rats flee at 1 HP each.
- **If they refuse combat:** **Stealth (DC 12)** or **Animal Handling (DC 13)** to scatter rats — still find tracks with Investigation DC 10.
- **If they ask for payment:** Garrick offers lantern only — coin comes from mayor in `11`.

### DM Notes

- **Pacing:** 15–20 minutes total — **optional**.
- **Mandatory:** **No** — tutorial only.
- **Mara Kell:** Missing hunter — notes in session 2 mines; do not resolve here.
- **Truth to hide:** Rats touched by **ambient wrongness** — not necromancer minions; no lecture.
- **Spells:** Only reference **`light_cantrip_evocation`** by slug if citing recharge.
- **Next scene:** `11_mayor_followup`.
