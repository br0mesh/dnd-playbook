# Mayor Follow-Up: Supplies and Forest Commitment

**Type:** Social consolidation · **Location:** Mayor Alden's hall (or hall steps at dusk) · **Pacing:** ~15–25 minutes

### Summary

**Purpose:** After **2–3 village nodes**, consolidate clues, push back if Alden claims **fake lanterns solved everything**, pay **up-front gp**, grant **map sketch** and rest until dawn, and confirm **minimum forest clues** before `12_forest_edge_closing`.

**Ends when:** Party accepts **5 gp each** (or negotiates), has **Fenwick's trail sketch**, chooses **rest until dawn**, and commits to the **old forest trail** — not necessarily leaving tonight.

**Minimum clue checklist (need any 2 forest-pointing threads):**

| Source | Clue |
|--------|------|
| **`09_herbalist_house`** | Diary — lights **answer each other**; source **not in village** |
| **`07_affected_homes`** | Coyle tracks + volunteer initials → **forest road** |
| **`10_mill_rat_problem`** | Mara's tracks → **forest edge** |
| **`06` / `09` volunteer layer** | Prior group followed **same trail** |

If table is short one clue, Fenwick adds: *"Silas's map and Coyle's tracks are the same mouth of trail — that's enough for me."*

### Read-aloud

**Return to the hall**

> Dusk settles on Briarford. **Mayor Alden** meets you on the hall **steps** — or inside if rain — with **Fenwick Cropp** behind him, maps rolled.
>
> "Well? Briarford's still standing. What did you find?"

**If they exposed fake lanterns in `08`**

> Alden's shoulders ease — too much. "Then it was **thieves**. Flash powder and cowards. Pay the cooper's lane no mind — we've **solved** it."
>
> He stops when you mention **blue ash**, **breath-pulse**, **Silas's diary**, or **volunteers**.

**Pushback read-aloud (any real clue)**

> Fenwick unrolls paper. "Mayor — **Coyle's ash** wasn't vinegar. **Silas** wrote **forest**. **Mara's** boots went **trees**. Thieves **copied** the real thing."
>
> Alden goes pale. "...Then it's not over."

### Checks

**Persuasion (DC 11)** — Keep Alden honest about payment

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| **Success** | **5 gp each** now; **25 gp split** on resolution reaffirmed. |
| **Fail** | Alden offers **3 gp each** — Fenwick adds 2 from clerk's purse on success **Persuasion DC 14** from another PC or Intimidation DC 12. |

**Insight (DC 10)** — Alden's relief about fakes

| Result | Read |
|--------|------|
| **Success** | He **wants** theft to be the whole story — fear, not cover-up. |
| **Fail** | He seems genuinely confused. |

#### Branch A — Party has diary (`09`)

- Alden reads excerpt over Fenwick's shoulder — hands shake on *"not in the village."*
- **Volunteer reinforcement:** "The boys who left the bedroll — they had **Silas's lane** written on their chit. Same trail."

#### Branch B — Party lacks diary but has `07` + mill or volunteers

- Fenwick connects dots aloud — grant **map sketch** anyway if at least **two** physical trails align.

**Investigation (DC 10)** — Fenwick's updated map

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Success** | Sketch: village → **old forest trail** → marked **"Silas X"** and **"mine?"** in margin — session 2 hook. |
| **Fail** | Fenwick reads directions aloud — same content. |

**Supplies (no roll — Alden provides)**

- **3 torches**, **1 healer's kit** (single use — flavor), **rations for 2 days**, permission to **rest at The Hearth & Horn** until **predawn**.

**Rest until dawn**

> The inn keeps a lamp low. Nobody sleeps well — porch lights **hiccup** once in the night (**Perception DC 14** wakes a watcher; others sleep). At **4 am**, the hall board creaks in wind. Time to walk.

**Clues to carry forward:**

- Alden acknowledges **real** crisis again.
- **Map sketch** to trail mouth.
- **Volunteers** and **Silas** same vector.
- **Payment** partial — stakes real.

**Convergence hooks:**

- Recap each PC intro thread aloud if players forgot — **whispers, breath, divine pull, herbs, guild stamp**.
- **Fake vs real** — Bram's confession vs Coyle ash — table should **own** the distinction before forest.

### Contingencies

- **If party skipped `08`:** Alden may not claim victory — skip pushback beat.
- **If party has zero forest clues:** Fenwick **refuses** map until they run **`09`** or **`07`** — do not end session 1 stuck.
- **If they demand full 25 gp now:** Alden refuses — Insight DC 10: village treasury is thin.
- **If they leave immediately:** Run **`12`** same night — valid but exhausting.
- **If they want to accuse Alden:** Insight clears him — evasive, not evil.

### DM Notes

- **Pacing:** 15–25 minutes — dialogue, payment, rest montage.
- **Mandatory:** Yes — gates **`12_forest_edge_closing`**.
- **Volunteer layer:** Reinforce bedroll, stablehand, initials — **bodies stay session 2**.
- **Truth to hide:** No soul reveal — Alden still thinks "curse or bandits."
- **Next scene:** `12_forest_edge_closing` at trail mouth after rest (or same dusk if table rushes).
