### Personality

An endlessly curious researcher with a hunger for knowledge who treats the world as one vast puzzle waiting to be solved.

### Backstory

Lilith graduated from a magical academy and now travels as a field scholar. She tracked unusual fluctuations in the ambient magical field to the woods near Briarford, hoping to harvest rare anomalous reagents, study the blue lantern light, and develop new spells from what she finds.

### Session 1 Introduction

**Spotlight scene:** *Blue Measurements* — the forest edge outside Briarford, well after midnight.

Lilith kneels in a ring of faintly luminescent herbs that only glow under the village lanterns' blue light. A crystal focus hovers in front of her, slowly turning as she records readings in a cramped notebook. The nearest lantern on a distant porch pulses in a rhythm that does not match wind, weather, or any natural source she has catalogued before.

She is not afraid yet. She is fascinated.

**What Lilith wants from the scene**

- **Gather blue-lantern starwort** from the ring before dawn bleaches the sap
- Measure whether the lantern's emission spikes when someone inside the house speaks or sleeps
- Find out whether the magical background noise is local to Briarford or part of something larger

**Gathering herbs**

- **Investigation (DC 12)** — cut the right stems on a blue pulse and preserve sap in vials (2 doses on success; 3 if you beat the DC by 5+)
- **Arcana (DC 13)** — alternative: pull samples when the field dips between pulses (2 doses on success)
- Careful RP with vials ready earns **1 dose** without a roll

**Possible checks**

- **Arcana (DC 11)** — before the flare: read the lantern's signature; success by 5+ warns you the next pulse will carry name-shaped information
- **Investigation (DC 12)** — trace whether the anomaly lives in the herbs, soil, or air
- **Perception (DC 13)** — a second blue lantern out of sync, or movement on the downslope road

**Complication**

While Lilith works, a villager's porch lantern flares brighter for a heartbeat and a whispered word rides the pulse — not loud enough to be speech, but shaped like a name. Her crystal focus cracks with a thin line of blue light.

- **Arcana (DC 12)** on the flare — fail: misread as harmless resonance, stay exposed, samples spoil faster; success: shield the focus, record the pattern, test a herb sample, or follow a second blue light

**Clues to leave behind**

- The herbs only luminesce under Briarford's blue lantern light, not ordinary flame
- The magical background here spikes toward the village, not away from it
- A porch lantern **whispered a name-shaped pulse** during the flare
- A folded contract notice is pinned to a trail marker near the road: Briarford is hiring help with the lanterns

**Intro ending**

> The crack in your focus is still warm when you look toward the village lights. Oiled cloth wraps your starwort samples. Whatever Briarford is broadcasting, it is worth more than a notebook page — and someone there is already paying for answers. You follow the road downhill to the first warm window: the tavern, and the mayor's hall beyond it.
