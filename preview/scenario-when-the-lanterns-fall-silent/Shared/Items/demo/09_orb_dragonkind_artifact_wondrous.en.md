# Orb of Dragonkind

### Header

**Name:** Orb of Dragonkind
**Rarity:** Artifact
**Type:** Wondrous item

### Properties

**Attunement:** Required
**Weight:** 3 lb.

### Description

An ancient orb attuned to a dragon type. While holding it, you can call on draconic power: advantage on saves vs. dragon breath, detect dragons within 30 ft/6 tiles, and invoke the orb's spells (DC 17) up to 7 charges.
