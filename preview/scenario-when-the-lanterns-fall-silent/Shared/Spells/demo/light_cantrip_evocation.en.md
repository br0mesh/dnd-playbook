# Light

### Header

**Name:** Light
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Evocation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, M (firefly or phosphorescent moss)
**Duration:** 1 hour

### Description

Touch one object no larger than 10 ft in any dimension. It sheds bright light 20 ft and dim light 20 ft beyond. Covering the object blocks the light.
