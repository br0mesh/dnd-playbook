# Swarm of Rats

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 1
**Wave:** Abandoned lair or collapsed tunnel
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)
**Stat source:** MM

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Surround and occupy the same space as a target; focus one PC if the party is split.
**Flee:** At half HP, the swarm breaks apart and flees through any Tiny opening.

---

### Stat Block

**Type:** Medium swarm of Tiny beasts, unaligned
**Armor Class AC:** 10
**Hit Points HP:** 24 (7d8 − 7)
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Senses:** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages:** —
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP)
**Damage Resistances:** bludgeoning, piercing, slashing
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, prone, restrained, stunned

### Main Stats

| Stat | Score | Modifier |
|---|--:|--:|
| Strength | 9 | −1 |
| Dexterity | 14 | +2 |
| Constitution | 9 | −1 |
| Intelligence | 2 | −4 |
| Wisdom | 10 | +0 |
| Charisma | 3 | −4 |

### Traits

- **Swarm.** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny rat. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

### Actions

| Action | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bites | +2 | 2d6 piercing | Melee, 0 ft.; half damage (1d6) if the swarm has half HP or fewer |

### DM Notes

- One swarm is a solid step up from **02_mature_rat_monster** for a level-1 tutorial fight.
- Describe movement as a rippling carpet of bodies — no need to track individual rats.
- Fire or area effects are effective; single-target attacks only affect one "layer" narratively but use normal rules.
