# Alter Self / Зміна обличчя

## English Version

### Header

**Name:** Alter Self
**Level:** 2nd
**School:** Transmutation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

### Description

Change appearance; natural weapons or aquatic adaptation.
