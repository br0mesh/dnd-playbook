# Arcane Lock / Магічний замок

## English Version

### Header

**Name:** Arcane Lock
**Level:** 2nd
**School:** Abjuration

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M
**Duration:** Until dispelled

### Description

Lock a door, window, or container; DC increases by 10.
