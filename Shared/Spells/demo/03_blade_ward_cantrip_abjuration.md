# Blade Ward / Клинковий щит

## English Version

### Header

**Name:** Blade Ward
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Abjuration

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 round

### Description

Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing until end of next turn.
