# Chill Touch / Морозний дотик

## English Version

### Header

**Name:** Chill Touch
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Necromancy

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 120 ft.
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 round

### Description

Ranged spell attack, 1d8 necrotic; target can't regain HP until start of your next turn.
