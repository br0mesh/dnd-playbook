# Thunderwave

### Header

**Name:** Thunderwave
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Evocation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self (15-ft. cube)
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

Creatures in a 15-ft. cube make a Constitution save or take 2d8 thunder damage and are pushed 10 ft.; loose objects are pushed.
