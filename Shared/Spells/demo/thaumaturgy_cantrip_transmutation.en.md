# Thaumaturgy

### Header

**Name:** Thaumaturgy
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Transmutation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 ft/6 tiles
**Components:** V
**Duration:** 1 minute

### Description

You manifest a minor wonder within range: your voice booms up to three times louder, flames flicker or brighten or dim, harmless tremors rumble the ground, an unlocked door or window flies open or slams shut, your eyes become solid black or glow for a moment, or a sound originates from a point you choose (such as a rumble of thunder or a sinister whisper).

If you cast this spell multiple times, you can have up to three of its 1-minute effects active at once; you can dismiss each effect as an action.
