# Scene 2: The Mill

### Summary

Investigation and optional combat with wolves in the mill yard.

### Read-aloud

> The mill wheel groans. Fresh claw marks score the door. A copper scent hangs in the air.

### Checks

- **Perception (DC 13):** Tracks leading to the cellar hatch.
- **Investigation (DC 12):** Merchant's satchel, bloodstained ledger.

### Contingencies

- **If they loud approach:** Wolves get surprise round.

### DM Notes

- Use monster sheets from Monsters section.
- Reward clever non-combat solutions with faction favor.
