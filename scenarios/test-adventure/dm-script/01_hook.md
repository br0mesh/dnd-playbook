# Scene 1: The Hook / Сцена 1: Зав'язка

## English Version

### Summary

Introduce the quest in a tavern; Marta mentions trouble at the mill.

### Read-aloud

> Rain hammers the roof. The fire pops. A nervous merchant whispers that something howls in the mill after midnight.

### Checks

- **Insight (DC 12):** Marta is hiding fear, not lying.
- **Persuasion (DC 10):** She shares the merchant's name.

### Contingencies

- **If they skip the tavern:** They find the mill tracks on the road.

### DM Notes

- Keep this scene under 10 minutes.
- Hand out quest letter if players miss clues.
