# Fake Lantern House: Thieves on Cooper's Lane

**Type:** Social investigation · optional short non-lethal fight · **Pacing:** ~20–30 minutes

### Summary

**Purpose:** Show that **some reports are fraud** — **Bram Keswick**, **Tessa Aldridge**, and **Old Henk** copied real blue flicker behaviour to scare neighbors off empty homes — while keeping the **central mystery intact**. Best run **after** `07_affected_homes` so players have seen real ash and breath-pulse.

**Ends when:** The party exposes the **fake mechanism**, gets a confession or clear evidence, and understands thieves **learned the trick from the real forest-road lanterns** — then leaves for `11_mayor_followup` or another node.

**Cast:**

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Bram Keswick** | Ringleader — cooper's apprentice, clever hands |
| **Tessa Aldridge** | Nervous accomplice — same Tessa from `07` if they met her |
| **Old Henk** | Fence — stores stolen goods in **Cooper's yard** shed |

### Read-aloud

**Cooper's lane**

> Smoke from the cooper's kiln, stack of staves, tar smell. **Old Henk's** shed leans against the yard fence — padlock **new**, hasp ** scratched** as if opened often.
>
> **Bram Keswick** crosses the yard with a lantern under his coat. **Tessa Aldridge** trails, eyes on the mud. They have not seen you yet.

**Social opening (preferred)**

> Bram freezes. "Mill business. Garrick's rats." The lantern under the coat **smells of vinegar and copper**, not lamp oil.

### Checks

**Investigation (DC 12)** — Shed or Bram's lantern (before or after talk)

| Result | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| **Fail** | Ordinary tools — if no other lead, allow **Perception DC 13** retry at shed. |
| **Success** | **Colored glass shards**, pinch of **alchemical powder**, oil **too clean** — staged blue flash. |
| **Success by 5+** | Bootprints match **local cobbles**, not forest mud — plus **ledger of emptied houses** in Henk's hand. |

**Insight (DC 11)** — Read Bram and Tessa

| Result | Read |
|--------|------|
| **Fail** | They look guilty of **something** — unclear what. |
| **Success** | **Petty thieves**, not cultists — sweat, not zeal. |
| **Success by 5+** | Tessa will **crack first** if separated. |

#### Branch A — Social resolution (preferred)

**Option 1 — Persuasion (DC 12) on Tessa**

- **Success:** Tessa blurts it out — Bram **saw real blue** near the **old forest trail** and copied the flicker to empty houses.
- **Failure:** Bram talks over her — try Intimidation or Investigation on shed.

**Option 2 — Intimidation (DC 13) on Bram**

- **Success:** "Fine. We **copied** what the forest road does. Real lights **whisper**; ours only **flash**. Henk moves goods."
- **Failure:** Bram runs for shed — may trigger Branch B.

**Option 3 — Deception (DC 13)** — pose as new mayor guards

- **Success:** Henk opens shed for "inventory" — stolen candlesticks, widow's shawl, **no** blue ash like Coyle's.
- **Failure:** Henk whistles; Bram bolts.

**Confession read-aloud (any success path):**

> "We aren't **making** people walk into trees. We saw the **real** porch lights go blue near the trail — **out of sync**, like breathing. We only wanted folk **scared enough to leave** silver behind. The **herbalist** and the **hunter** went toward the forest for **real** reasons. We aren't that stupid."

#### Branch B — Short non-lethal fight

Run only if players charge or Bram runs.

| Enemy | Stat note |
|-------|-----------|
| **Bram Keswick** | AC 12, HP 9, shortsword +3 (1d6+1) |
| **Tessa Aldridge** | AC 11, HP 5, throws brick +2 (1d4) then **surrenders** at half HP |
| **Old Henk** | AC 10, HP 6, **dodge** — yields if Bram down |

- **Goal:** Subdue, not kill — Henk whines for mercy.
- **After 2 rounds:** Tessa drops brick — "It's fake, it's **fake**, the forest ones aren't!"

**Investigation (DC 10)** on fake lantern mechanism (post-fight or social)

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Success** | Powder + glass = **one-second blue** — no whisper, no ash, no breath rhythm. |
| **Fail** | Players still get confession if Tessa talked. |

**Clues to carry forward:**

- Fakes **copied real forest-road behaviour** — proves phenomenon predates thieves.
- Local panic spreads faster than Alden admits.
- **Silas** and **Mara** did not leave for stolen candlesticks — real trail still open.
- Tessa connects **`07`** neighbor beat to **`08`** guilt — optional drama.

**Convergence hooks:**

- **Out-of-sync flicker** — Bram admits copying what **Lilith / Ruta / Mordain** saw.
- **Name whispers** — fakes **cannot** reproduce whispers — **Vex / Lilith** distinction.
- **Real ash at Coyle cottage** — contrast with vinegar smell here.

### Contingencies

- **If party never came to `07`:** Bram still confesses real vs fake on successful Persuasion — real mystery stays.
- **If they murder Henk:** Mayor revokes credit at inn — heavy social cost; investigation continues but tone shifts.
- **If they skip this node:** Alden may claim "theft explains everything" in `11` — pushback with `07` evidence.
- **If Tessa was friendly in `07`:** Insight DC 10 — she joined Bram from fear, not malice.

### DM Notes

- **Pacing:** 20–30 minutes social; +10 if fight.
- **Optional:** Useful for deduction tables; **not** required for forest commit.
- **Truth to hide:** Thieves are **not** necromancer agents — no bound-soul reveal.
- **Tessa Aldridge:** Same NPC as nervous neighbor in `07` — optional continuity.
- **Next scene:** `11_mayor_followup` after 2–3 nodes, or `09_herbalist_house` if not yet run.
