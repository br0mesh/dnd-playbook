# Mage Hand

### Header

**Name:** Mage Hand
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Transmutation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 ft
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 minute

### Description

Spectral floating hand. Manipulate objects up to 10 lb within 30 ft; cannot attack, activate magic items, or carry more than 10 lb.
