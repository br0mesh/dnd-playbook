# Find Familiar

### Header

**Name:** Find Familiar
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Conjuration

### Casting

**Time:** 1 hour
**Range:** 10 ft.
**Components:** V, S, M
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

Summon a spirit that takes the form of a tiny beast; acts independently; can deliver touch spells within 100 ft.
