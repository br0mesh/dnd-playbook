# Wolf (Ash Eyes)

---

### Encounter Reference

**Default quantity:** 3
**Wave:** Collapsed Coal Road — ash-eyed wolf pack
**Challenge Rating CR:** 1/4 (50 XP each)
**Stat source:** MM Wolf, cosmetic only

### Quick Tactics

**Behavior:** Circle downwind; one alpha lunges when the party is spread on the ruined road. Pack tactics per MM — advantage on attacks when an ally is within 5 ft of the target.
**Flee:** When two wolves are down or the alpha drops below half HP — survivors break toward the treeline, not the mine.

### DM Notes

- Scene: `14_collapsed_coal_road` — forest approach to Briarford coal mines.
- **Scaling:** 3 for 4–5 PCs (default); 2 for 3 PCs; 4 only if the party is fresh and wants a harder road fight.
- **Cosmetic only:** eyes dull **ash-grey** with faint ember glow — same ambient wrongness as mill rats (`01_giant_rat_ember_eyes_monster`), not necromancer minions.
- **Teaching beat:** Pack Tactics and prone rider on bite — one rules lesson, then move on.
- **Non-combat:** Animal Handling DC 13 or group Intimidation DC 12 with torches — wolves withdraw without full fight.
- **If fight drags:** Alpha flees at half HP; remaining wolves follow.
