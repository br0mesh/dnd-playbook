### DM Notes

- Former runner from the **Ashmere lot** — scared, drunk, testing whether Vex is still alive. **Not** a bounty hunter (truth on failed Insight too).
- Shares 1–2 clues if Vex talks outside: crew vanished over a fortnight; city coin on sealed records; porch lantern **whispered a name** (carter rumor); ledger copies tied to **Briarford toll-road**.
- Optional combat: knife, AC 11, 7 HP — goal is escape, not a boss fight. **Mira** ejects both if a brawl starts.
- Do **not** reveal Ashmere's full fate or necromancer truth — incomplete, scared knowledge only.
- Will **not** follow Vex into Briarford. If killed, murder-warrant flavor pushes Vex toward the village urgently.
