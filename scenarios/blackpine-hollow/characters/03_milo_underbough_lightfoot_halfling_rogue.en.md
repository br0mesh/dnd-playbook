### Special Abilities

- **Appearance** — Small, bright-eyed, curly brown hair, many pockets, always holding a snack.
- **Personality** — Curious, funny, too confident near locked things.
- **Goal** — Find a story impressive enough that nobody calls him "just lucky" again.
- **Fear / Weakness** — Pretends not to be scared, then talks too much.
- **Backstory** — Milo has traveled from village to village as a performer, card player, and occasional finder of misplaced valuables. He insists he is an adventurer, though most of his adventures started as accidents. He joined the camping trip to entertain friends and maybe find a little harmless treasure. The glowing lanterns are exactly the kind of trouble he claims he was born for.
- **Sneak Attack** — Once per turn, deal +2d6 damage if you have advantage or an ally is next to the target.
- **Cunning Action** — Use a bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide.
- **Fast Hands** — Use a bonus action for thieves' tools, opening objects, or quick item tricks.
- **Halfling Luck** — If you roll a 1 on a d20, reroll it once.
- **Brave** — Advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
- **Naturally Stealthy** — Can try to hide behind a creature larger than Milo.
