# Healing Word

### Header

**Name:** Healing Word
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Evocation

### Casting

**Time:** 1 bonus action
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

A creature of your choice within range regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

### At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.
