# Hunter's Mark

### Header

**Name:** Hunter's Mark
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Divination

### Casting

**Time:** 1 bonus action
**Range:** 90 ft.
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

### Description

Mark a creature you can see within range as your quarry. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra 1d6 weapon damage to the target whenever you hit it with a weapon attack, and you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) and Wisdom (Survival) checks to find it. If the target drops to 0 hit points before the spell ends, you can use a bonus action to mark a new creature.

### At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a 3rd- or 4th-level slot, you can maintain concentration for up to 8 hours. With a 5th-level slot or higher, you can maintain it for up to 24 hours.
