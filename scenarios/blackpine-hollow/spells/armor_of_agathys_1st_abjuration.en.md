# Armor of Agathys

### Header

**Name:** Armor of Agathys
**Level:** 1st
**School:** Abjuration

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S, M (a cup of water)
**Duration:** 1 hour

### Description

A protective magical frost surrounds you. You gain 5 temporary hit points. While those hit points last, a creature that hits you with a melee attack takes 5 cold damage.

### At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a slot of 2nd level or higher, both the temporary hit points and the cold damage increase by 5 for each slot level above 1st.
