# Spare the Dying

### Header

**Name:** Spare the Dying
**Level:** Cantrip
**School:** Necromancy

### Casting

**Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

### Description

Touch a living creature that has 0 hit points. The creature becomes stable. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
