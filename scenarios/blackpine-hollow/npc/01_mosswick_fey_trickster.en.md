# Mosswick — Fey Trickster

---

### Scene Reference

**Default quantity:** 1
**Appears in:** Ruined shrine (Scene 7) through endings
**Challenge Rating CR:** 0 (social encounter)
**Stat source:** Homebrew

### Quick Roleplay

**Voice:** Playful, childish, creepy — grin too wide
**Goal:** Freedom, attention, fair hearing before judgment
**Behavior:** Talks first; mocks gently; bargains when hurt; not fight-to-death unless cornered

### Sample Lines

| Line | When |
|---|---|
| "Campers! Heroes! Snack-carriers! You made it." | First meeting - opens the social scene |
| "They locked me under stone and called it peace." | Explaining history - shrine backstory |
| "I borrowed names, voices, paths. Small things. Mostly." | Defending actions - shows he is not purely evil |
| "Tell it right. Tell them I was here." | If sealed fairly - fair seal ending |

### DM Notes

- **Scene anchor:** Ruined shrine (Scene 7) through endings; 1 social NPC; CR 0; homebrew reference entry.
- **Stat snapshot:** Small fey, chaotic neutral; Speed 30 ft.; Languages Common, Sylvan.
- **Wants:** Freedom and recognition that the shrine prison was cruel; games, attention, someone to tell his story truthfully; not to be sealed again without being heard.
- **Fears:** Eternal imprisonment; being ignored or treated as a monster with no feelings.
- **Bargain terms:** Returns stolen names, voices, paths, and memories; protects travelers in Blackpine Hollow; harmless tricks only; answers when lantern spirits call; party tells his story truthfully.
- **Misty Escape:** If combat starts, use the monster entry and let him teleport away once below half HP.
- Keep Mosswick unsettling but not purely evil.
